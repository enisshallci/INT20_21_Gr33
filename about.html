WHAT SETS US APART


Welcome to Wildland Trekking! We offer premier hiking vacations across the U.S.A. and the globe. Many adventure companies focus on multiple sports, but not us – we’re 100% focused on the trekking experience. With exceptional guides, fantastic meals, top-of-the-line gear, dialed-in logistics, incredible support staff, and award-winning itineraries, we are passionate about providing unforgettable trekking experiences for guests all over the world! We know well that among people who love to hike, there is a wide range of preference in terms of the type of hiking and/or accommodations on a trip. Our hiking and backpacking tours are all-inclusive so guests can relax and focus 100% on enjoying their vacation.

WHAT SETS US APART


Our commitment is to hire and train the best guides in the industry. We seek out career-minded guides with extensive personal and professional wilderness experience, as well as hard skills and certifications. Perhaps most important though is that Wildland guides are knowledgeable, friendly, helpful and passionate.When possible, we prefer to get our groups not only far away from roads and crowds, but even off hiking trails. This type of intimate wilderness travel is most conducive to small groups, with often max of 5 or 6 guests, and at most 12.We provide gear; local transportation (with a few exceptions); meals; necessary permits; reservations and fees; natural and cultural history interpretation and more!
